Artificial Intelligence

01. Core Definition

What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence is the development of computer systems designed to perform tasks typically requiring human intelligence. This includes learning from data, recognizing patterns, and understanding natural language.

02. Foundations

Technological Basis

AI leverages advanced techniques to solve complex problems:

  • Machine Learning: Algorithms that improve through experience.
  • Deep Learning: Neural networks mimicking the human brain.
03. Future & Ethics

Outlook & Responsibility

As AI transforms industries, focus must shift to ethical considerations: privacy protection, bias mitigation, and responsible deployment to ensure safety and equity.
