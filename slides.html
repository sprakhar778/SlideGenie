The Evolution of Artificial Intelligence

01

Foundation:
Early Rule-Based Systems

Defined by explicit programming and rigid logic, these initial systems operated on pre-defined 'if-then' rules to solve specific, structured problems.

02

Progression:
The Evolutionary Shift

Tracing the development of intelligence involved moving away from static instructions toward dynamic adaptation and machine learning concepts.

03

Current State:
Modern Deep Learning

Today's AI utilizes multi-layered neural networks to process vast amounts of unstructured information, mimicking human cognitive patterns.
