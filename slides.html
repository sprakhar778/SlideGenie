The Evolution of Artificial Intelligence

From static rules to dynamic neural networks
1

Foundation:
Rule-Based Systems

Defined by explicit programming and rigid logic, operating on pre-defined 'if-then' rules to solve structured problems.

2

Progression:
The Evolutionary Shift

Tracing the development of intelligence involved moving away from static instructions toward dynamic adaptation.

3

Current State:
Deep Learning

Today's AI utilizes multi-layered neural networks to process vast amounts of unstructured information.
